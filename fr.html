France

"Les Bleus"

Coach: Didier Deschamps

Formation: 4-3-3

Star Player: Karim Benzema

France were expected to be one of the last nations standing, and while they did make it to the quarter-finals, they simply did not have enough firepower to get past Germany. They were overall mediocre, only managing to beat team they were expected to and were overall unable to beat superior sides that could pose problems like Germany did. France saw inspired form from star striker Karim Benzema along with a 21 year old Paul Pogba dominating the midfield.
