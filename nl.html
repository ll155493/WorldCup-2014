The Netherlands

"Oranje"

Coach: Louis van Gaal

Formation: 4-3-1-2

Star Player: Arjen Robben

The Netherlands made it as far the semi-final, going out of the tournament at the hands of Argentina. They did however manage to win the third place medal defeating Brazil in the third place match 3-0. While they did not win it all, The Netherlands had several historic moments throughout the tournament, most notably Robin van Persie's header goal versus Spain in the group stage, remembered as the "Flying Dutchman" goal. Their tournament saw flashes of great attacking display from the front duo of Robben and van Persie, but they couldn't find the goal against Argentina needed to get them to the final.
