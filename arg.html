Argentina

"La Albiceleste"

Coach: Alejandro Sabella

Star Player: Lionel Messi

Formation: 4-4-1-1

After eliminating the likes of Belgium and The Netherlands, Messi & co went onto to nearly win the entire tournament, just failing to beat Germany to the trophy in the final match. The final went to extra time, as neither team could manage to best the other with a goal in regulation time, unfortunately for Argentina, Germany found the goal that was needed to be crowned champions before Argentina could. Their run saw key performances from none other than Lionel Messi with four goal contributions for his side, helped on by a solid defensive unit that held opposition scoreless on four separate occasions.
