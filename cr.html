Costa Rica

"Los Ticos"

Coach: Jorge Luis Pinto

Formation: 5-4-1

Star Player: Keylor Navas

The true Cinderella story of the tournament, Costa Rica shocked the world by making it all the way to the quarter-finals where they were elminated by The Netherlands in a penalty shootout nail-biter. Entering the tournament with no notable stars, Jorge Luis Pinto's men made up for what they lacked in quality with passion and tenacity. This 2014 run saw goalkeeper Keylor Navas gain the attention of big European clubs, who went on to have an amazing club career off the back of his World Cup display, most notably as starting keeper for Real Madrid.
