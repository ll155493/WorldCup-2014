CHAMPIONS

The champions of the 2014 World Cup won in dramatic fashion. From their 7-1 pummeling of Brazil in the semi-final, to their last gasp over time goal from Mario Götze to win the World Cup over Argentina, Germany won with style, emotion, and all the talent needed to go with becoming champions. While Thomas Müller showed out as one of the best attackers of the tournament, you simply cannot narrow this group down to one 'best' individual, from Bayern captain Philip Lamm to legend Bastian Schweinsteiger, Germany had a collective unit of world class players that each played a massive role in them lifting the sport's most prestigious trophy in 2014.
