Brazil

"Seleção"

Coach: Luiz Felipe Scolari

Formation: 4-2-3-1

Brazil came out of the gate hot in 2014 and were looking to be a formiddable foe for any team that would have to cross their path. That was until they faced off against Germany and found themselves on the wrong end of, one of, if not the most historic defeat in World Cup history, losing 7-1 in the semi-finals. Losing star players like Thiago Silva and Neymar due to injury didn't help their chances in the late stages of the tournament.
