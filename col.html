Colombia

"Los Cafeteros"

Coach: José Pékerman

Formation: 4-4-2

Star Player: James Rodriguez

Although they were viewed as a solid group, not much was expected of Colombia entering the 2014 World Cup. Instead, they made it all the way to the quarter-finals (eliminated by Brazil) on the back of sublime form from Golden Boot winner James Rodriguez with six goals and two assists, widely considered the best player of the entire tournament. He was accompanied by a cast of players who filled their roles effectively.
